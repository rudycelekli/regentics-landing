Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you delegate your first task.

Is this just another chatbot?

No. Regentics agents have roles, budgets, and tool access. They open issues, write code, publish posts, and update your CRM — without you babysitting every prompt.

How do approval gates work?

You configure what actions require your sign-off (e.g., spend >$100, publish to LinkedIn, hire an agent). The agent pauses, notifies you, and resumes once approved.

Can I connect my own tools?

Yes. GitHub, LinkedIn, Instagram, Reddit, OpenAI, and more are available via the Adapter Manager. Custom adapters are supported too.

Who owns the work product?

You do. Everything lives in your company scope: your repos, your accounts, your CRM. We are the control plane, not the owner.

How fast can I see real output?

Most teams see their first deliverable within 2 minutes of setup: a drafted post, a researched lead list, or a pushed code change.
